Lesson 1: About Me & School

Today's Goal

Speak for 40-50 seconds about yourself and your school

Use at least 6 sentences with 2 "because" explanations

Teacher's Tips

  • Speak clearly and at a comfortable pace
  • Use complete sentences
  • Don't worry about mistakes - just try!

Common Questions & Answers

Personal Questions

What's your name? 15 sec

Model answer: My name is Zhang Xiaoming. You can call me Mike.

Tell me about your family. 30 sec

Model answer: There are four people in my family: my parents, my sister and me. We are a close family.

School Questions

Do you like your school? Why? 20 sec

Model answer: Yes, I do. My school is clean and friendly. The teachers are helpful and I have many friends.

What's your teacher like? 25 sec

Model answer: My English teacher is kind and funny. She always makes our lessons interesting with games.
