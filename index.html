Part 3: Dialogue (Family)

T: Tell me about your family.
S: There are [4] people in my family. I live with my [parents]. We are a [close] family.
T: Do you have any brothers or sisters?
S: Yes, I have a [younger brother]. He is [8] years old.

Part 3: Dialogue (School)

T: What is your favourite subject? Why?
S: My favourite subject is [English] because [it is useful].
T: What do you do after school?
S: After school, I often [go to a club]. Then I [do homework].
T: Ask them to change "English" to "Maths" or "PE".
