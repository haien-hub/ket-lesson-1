Welcome to Your KET Speaking Lesson!

Today we will practice talking about yourself and your school.

Lesson Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Introduce yourself (name, age, family, where you live)
  • Talk about your school (name, subjects, teachers, activities)
  • Speak for 40-50 seconds without stopping
  • Use "because" to explain your reasons

Let's Get Started!

Teacher: Please ask the student to introduce themselves. Encourage full sentences.

Hello! My name is __________. I'm __________ years old. I live in __________.

Sentence Practice

Practice the key sentence patterns for KET speaking test

Key Sentence Patterns

Teacher: Read each sentence pattern aloud, then ask the student to complete it with their own information.

1. My name is __________. / You can call me __________.

2. I'm __________ years old.

3. I live with __________ in __________.

4. I'm a student at __________ School.

5. My favourite subject is __________ because __________.

6. After school, I often __________.

High-Score Expressions

These expressions can help you get a higher score in the speaking test.

My full name is __________, but you can call me __________.

What I like most is __________ because __________.

I feel __________ when __________.

Speaking Tips:

  • Start with your name and age
  • Talk about where you live and your family
  • Describe your school and favourite subject
  • Mention what you do after school
  • Use "because" to explain why you like something
