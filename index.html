T1: About Me & School

25-minute Lesson

Target Student: KET Exam Candidate

Master the vocabulary and sentence structures needed to talk about personal information and school life for 40-50 seconds

Let's start speaking!

Teacher Instructions:

1. Start with a warm greeting: "Hello! Welcome to our KET speaking class!"

2. Briefly introduce today's topic: "Today we'll learn to talk about ourselves and our school."

3. Ask the student: "Are you ready to practice speaking?"

Lesson Task

Today's Core Task

Speaking Goal

Speak for 40-50 seconds about "About Me & School"

Sentence Requirements

Use at least 6 sentences with minimum 2 "because" explanations

Success Criteria

Clear pronunciation, correct grammar, relevant vocabulary, logical structure

Lesson Structure

Part 1: About Me (5 min)

Personal information: name, age, family, home

Part 2: About School (10 min)

School information: subjects, teachers, activities

Part 3: Practice (10 min)

Role play, timed speaking, feedback

Error Correction & Feedback

Common Mistakes Summary

Vocabulary Confusion

Mistake: Using "live in" instead of "live with" for people

Correct: "I live with my parents." (NOT "I live in my parents")

Correct: "I live in Beijing." (FOR places)

Grammar Error

Mistake: "There is 4 people in my family." (wrong verb agreement)

Correct: "There are 4 people in my family." (plural = are)

Remember: "There is" for singular, "There are" for plural

Pronunciation Issue

Mistake: Saying "subjec" instead of "subject" (dropping final "t")

Correct: "subject" (with clear "t" sound at the end)

Practice: "subject, student, important" (all have final "t")

Missing "because"

Mistake: "My favourite subject is English. It's interesting." (two separate sentences)

Better: "My favourite subject is English because it's interesting." (one sentence with reason)

Remember: You need at least 2 "because" in your 40-50 second speech
